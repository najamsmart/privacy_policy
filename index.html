Privacy Policy for Match and Learn

Effective Date: 14 May 2025

Developer: Game Circuit

Email: gamecircuitdeveloper@gmail.com

1. Introduction

At Match and Learn, your child’s privacy is our top priority. Our game is designed for toddlers and preschoolers and fully complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and GDPR-K.

This game requires no login, account creation, or personal information collection. Just download and start learning!

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. The game does not request names, contact info, emails, phone numbers, location data, photos, videos, voice input, or payment information.

3. Advertisements

Match and Learn uses Unity Ads to serve non-personalized, child-safe ads. These ads are configured for child-directed treatment in compliance with COPPA and GDPR-K. Unity may process technical, non-identifiable data to ensure proper ad delivery but does not collect personal data for ad targeting. For more information, see Unity Privacy Policy.

4. Anonymous Usage Data

We do not collect usage data or analytics. The game runs entirely on your device; no gameplay information or crash reports are sent to any server.

5. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.

6. Contact Us

For privacy questions or concerns, contact us at gamecircuitdeveloper@gmail.com.
